We often talk about food as fuel, but food is also relationship: to our bodies, to each other, and to the living world.

Nutrition is not separate from lived experience. Every meal arrives with context: time, money, stress, culture, family, access, memory, hunger, fatigue, and belief.

When we understand food only as calories, we miss the rest of the conversation.
