We often talk about food as fuel, but food is also relationship: to our bodies, to each other, and to the living world. In this series, I explore how nutrition, attention, and environment weave into a coherent practice—so change can feel less like willpower and more like alignment.

## Try-this reflection

- Where did your last meal come from? How many hands did it pass through?
- What changed in your body after eating—energy, mood, digestion?
- What one tiny tweak (environment, routine, story) would make the next meal calmer?
